Privacy Policy for Connect Printer

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Connect Printer ("the app") is used to configure compatible printers over Bluetooth and Wi-Fi.

Data collection

  • The app does not create user accounts.
  • The app does not sell personal data.
  • The app does not transmit personal data to our servers.
  • The app does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Permissions used

  • Bluetooth permissions: used to discover and connect to nearby printers.
  • Location (Android 6-11 only): required by Android system behavior for Bluetooth discovery.
  • Wi-Fi state permissions: used to assist Wi-Fi configuration flow for the printer.
  • Internet permission: reserved for compatibility; no user analytics or ad network traffic is intentionally used by the app.

How data is handled

  • Printer configuration details (such as SSID and password) are entered by the user and sent directly to the printer over a local Bluetooth connection.
  • This information is not intentionally uploaded to a backend controlled by us.

Data retention

  • The app does not maintain a remote database of user data.
  • Any temporary processing happens locally on the device.

Children

The app is not directed to children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app publisher through the contact method listed in the Google Play listing.
